About Covid-19:-



-On 7 January, Scientists first reported on a mysterious illness emerging in China. At least 59 people had become ill with the disease, and seven were in a critical condition.



The official names for the Disease was announced as The Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) on the contrary the Virus was called Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2).



Thanks to fast and collaborative work by scientists, we learned about the biology of the virus, how it spreads, who is most at risk of disease and the symptoms people get. That knowledge helped to guide public policy and form a basis for action on a massive and global scale.



The research continues apace, and even though the virus had infected more than 39 million people and claimed in excess of 1.1 million lives worldwide, and still there are many things we still don’t know about this coronavirus and the disease it causes
